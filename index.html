ABOUT US

Proudly representing the Tamworth Region

With a history dating back more than 100 years, the Tamworth & District Highland Society Pipe Band is a community focused band dedicated to performing and teaching traditional Scottish music in the Tamworth region.

We perform annually at events such as the Country Music Festival Parade, the Liverpool Plains Military Tattoo, ANZAC Day, the Aberdeen Highland Games and The Brain Foundation Xmas Fair.

If you would like to be part of the band, or would like us to perform at your event, contact us today.
